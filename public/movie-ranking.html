Lea Recommends

My favorite movies

The Last Unicorn

Sure it's popular now, but I'm an OG fan. The story is wonderful and the soundtrack by America is still exceptional.

Secret of NIMH

The best film Don Bluth Studios produced. Startlingly beautiful animation, a nice soundtrack, and the climax is a robot test.

Flight of Dragons

Incredibly detaild artwork when you remember this was all done by hand. Looks like an animated baroque tapestry, and the story is pretty good too.
